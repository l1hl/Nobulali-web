Welcome to ShakeXperience

ShakeXperience, is an Applied Theatre company best known for award-winning educational programmes. The company has developed a teaching method known as ShakeXperience®, providing theatre productions and experiential learning for those in schools and corporate settings.

ShakeXperience ® will design, implement, and oversee educational programmes to meet the client’s needs.

ShakeXperience ® is also excited to take on facilitation activities and consulting for your organisation. Dr. Nobulali is the lead Facilitator, who has garnered invaluable experience along her path, including co-founding Nudgeathon™. Together with a team of experts, she designs, implements, and evaluates programmes and research for organisations.
